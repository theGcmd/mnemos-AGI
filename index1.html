01 / The mission

Today's AI does not think. It predicts the next word using a giant math signal pushed through the whole network at once. Brains do not work like that. A brain learns by neurons reacting to the neurons next to them, and somehow that becomes intelligence. I am building an AI the brain way. No global signals. Only local rules. No one has shown this can scale to real reasoning. That is the point.
